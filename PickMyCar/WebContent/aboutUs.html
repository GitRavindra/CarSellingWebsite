About

The system is an online car buying system. This system is a solution for buying cars online.
In other words a tool that enhances the customer experience. It assists in organizing and simplifying the activities of a customer for purchasing a car.
It presents all the information to the customer in a well formatted and organised form in the comfort of his/her home. It drastically reduces the customers cost of buying a new car by cutting unnecessary costs and middle men out of the process.
It further sweetens the deal by reducing the time a customer would require to buy a new car exponentially. It is an avant garde idea that will be implemented at a national level and customer testing indicates that it will be well received by the potential buyers..

Purpose

1. This is the system works on a market space( i.e internet) instead of a market place.
2. This is the system which saves customers time.. It would be decrease the price of cars and make the dream of owning a car possible for common man.. It allows the customer to compare cars of different brands and models in fields such engine type, torque, speed transmission, etc.

Aim

The main aim of the project is to analyse the requirements of the customers and provide them an interface as a website, so users can buy cars easily.

Objective

a) To provide a pragmatic system for buying new cars in a hassle freeway.
b) To reduce the prices for the customer by improving the system and increasing its efficiency.
c) To save the customers time he/she wastes going to different showrooms and waiting in lines by taking the entire process online.
d) To increase the level of comfort of the customer by allowing him/her to access the feature anywhere in their phones or computers.
